The Problem

NYC's civic data is public.
But no one can reach it.

Critical data on housing, workers, and city AI tools sits buried in spreadsheets and agency PDFs. Accessing it requires SQL skills most citizens don't have — so they can't ask questions, and can't hold the city accountable.

The Solution

Talk to the data.
Get real answers.

  • Voice-first — Gemini Live speech-to-speech. No typing. No menus. Just ask.
  • Grounded in reality — every answer backed by actual NYC open datasets via MCP + DuckDB. Zero hallucination.
  • Three outputs simultaneously — voice answer, precise chart, live borough map. All under 20 seconds.
  • Three civic lenses — AI transparency, mortgage access, gig worker economics.
Multimodal — "See, Hear, and Speak"

Three outputs. One question.
